Characteristics of Life

  • Made up of at least one cell
  • Can reproduce
  • Has DNA
  • Uses energy (metabolism)
  • Responds to stimulus from the environment
  • Grows and develops
  • Maintains homeostasis
  • Evolves (change over time as a group)

Homeostasis

Homeostasis is when an organism maintains a near-constant internal tempreature, normally so that important chemical reactions have the chance to occur.
